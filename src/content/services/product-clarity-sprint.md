title: "Product Clarity Sprint"
tagline: "From idea to plan, in two weeks."
price_from: "£3,200"
duration: "2 weeks"
scope: "Fixed scope"
order: 4

who_for:
  - "Founders with an idea and a developer (or budget for one) but no plan"
  - "Small businesses about to commission a custom build and unsure what they're commissioning"
  - "Product teams pivoting or launching a new line and needing to scope it cleanly"
  - "Anyone who's been told 'we just need to start building' by someone who shouldn't be making that call"

who_not_for:
  - "Companies with an existing product and a messy backlog — that's Backlog Rescue"
  - "Teams three weeks from launch with no plan — that's Launch Readiness Sprint"
  - "Anyone wanting a 60-page strategy document"

what_you_get:
  - label: "Problem and user definition"
    description: "Who this is for, what they're actually trying to do, and what they're using today. Short, specific, not a persona deck."
  - label: "MVP scope"
    description: "The smallest version that delivers value. What's in, what's out, what's later."
  - label: "Prioritised feature list"
    description: "Ordered by value and effort, with a rationale that survives stakeholder pressure."
  - label: "30/60/90-day roadmap"
    description: "Phased plan with milestones tied to outcomes, not feature counts."
  - label: "Risks and assumptions log"
    description: "What might kill this, what you're betting on, what to validate first."
  - label: "Dev-ready brief"
    description: "A document a developer or agency can quote against without follow-up questions."

outcomes:
  - "Half the feature list cut before any money is spent building it"
  - "A clear 'no' answer to at least one expensive idea"
  - "A scope that fits the actual budget"
  - "A founder who can describe the product in one sentence"

steps:
  - label: "Days 1–3 — Discovery"
    description: "Working sessions to understand the business, the user, and what you're really trying to build. Existing materials reviewed."
  - label: "Days 4–7 — Scoping"
    description: "MVP shaped. Features prioritised. Cuts proposed and discussed."
  - label: "Days 8–10 — Roadmap"
    description: "Phased plan built. Risks logged. Dev-ready brief drafted."
  - label: "Days 11–14 — Handover"
    description: "Final document delivered. Walk-through call. One revision round."
